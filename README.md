# Sales Performance & Customer Insights Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Python. The goal is to understand business performance through sales trends over time, category performance, regional performance, and customer segment behavior.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Dataset
The dataset contains order-level sales information, including order date, category, region, customer segment, and sales value.

## Analysis Performed
- Sales trend over time
- Sales by category
- Sales by region
- Sales by customer segment

## Key Findings
- Sales increased significantly after 2016 and remained high in later years
- Technology is the highest-performing category
- The West region generates the highest sales
- The Consumer segment contributes the most revenue

## Business Insight
The business performs best in technology products, western regions, and consumer markets. These areas appear to be the strongest drivers of revenue and may deserve continued strategic focus.
